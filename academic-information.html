Academic Information

General requirement to gain admission.

All applicants for undergraduate degrees should posses the following credentials/Documents:

  • SSCE/GCE/O'LEVEL/NABTEB or it's equivalent at not more than two sittings, including Engish Language and Mathematics
  • Age Declaration or
  • Birth certificate
  • 6 passport sized photographs(white background)
  • Photocopy of all available results/certificate
  • JAMB is not needed

All applicants for master degrees should posses the following credentials/Documents:

  • Undergraduate transcripts
  • Original first degree result
  • 6 passport sized photographs(white background)
  • Birth certificate

Transfer Students:

  • Transcripts from former institution or university
  • Age Declaration or
  • Birth certificate
  • 6 passport sized photographs(white background)
  • Photocopy of all available results/certificate
